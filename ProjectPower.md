<div style="text-align: center;">
<p style="font-size: 20pt;">Project Power</p>
<p style="font-size: 15pt;">A 3D Beat 'Em Up Indie Game</p>
</div>

***Developed With: C++, Unreal, Blueprints, Blender***\
***Estimated Release: Late 2026 / Early 2027***

What started off as a learning exercise in 3D game development eventually grew into a full game project. The game is a third-person action game that takes inspiration from 3D beat 'em ups. In keeping with the theme of being a learning exercise, all code and models (model, rigging, animations) was created from scratch by me.

<div style="text-align: center;">
<p style="font-size: 15pt;">Features</p>
</div>

<p style="font-size: 12pt;">Multiple Playable Characters</p>
7 Playable characters (3 starting and 4 unlockable) are available. Each one has a unique playstyle so no two characters feel the same to play.  


<p style="font-size: 12pt;">Multiple Weapons and Loadouts</p>
Each character has up to 10 unlockable weapons and can bring 4 weapons into a stage (2 loadouts, 2 weapons per loadout). Like the characters, each weapon offers a unique style of gameplay


<p style="font-size: 12pt;">Flexible juggle-based combo system</p>
Weapons and loadouts can be freely swapped mid combo, to give you flexibility in how you want your combo to flow. May weapons come with a standard multi-move combo that can be cancelled into other weapons or into a dodge to allow you to keep the pressure going and create fighting game-esque combos.


<p style="font-size: 12pt;">Numerous defensive mechanics</p>
Freely defend yourself in any way you want. Stand your ground with a defensive guard, or nimbly dodge around enemy attacks , or if you time it right parry an enemy attack to give yourself an offensive advantage.


<p style="font-size: 12pt;">40+ levels, 20+ Enemy Types, and 10 Bosses</p>
Brawl your way across 40+ levels facing off against ever changing enemy diversity and challenge 10 bosses across your playthorugh.
